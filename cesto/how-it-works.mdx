## Connect your wallet

Head to [app.cesto.co](https://app.cesto.co) and connect your Solana wallet. Cesto uses WalletConnect, so any compatible wallet works. No sign-ups, no accounts — your wallet is your identity.

## Browse baskets

Each basket is a curated portfolio built around a specific theme or narrative. Browse what's available, read the thesis, check the assets inside, and see historical performance.

Every basket shows you:
- The narrative it's built around
- The assets it holds and their weights
- Backtested annual returns based on historical data

<Note>
Performance figures shown on baskets reflect backtested historical data from the past year. They represent how the basket's strategy would have performed, not guaranteed future returns.
</Note>

## Invest

Found a basket that matches your conviction? Hit invest. Cesto handles the complexity — multiple assets, multiple swaps, all executed atomically in a single transaction. You don't need to buy each asset individually.

Once the transaction confirms, the underlying assets are in your wallet. Not a receipt. Not a token representing a basket. The actual assets.

## Stay aligned

Markets move. Allocations drift. Baskets are regularly updated to reflect the latest strategy, and Cesto gives you the tools to keep your positions in line. Head over to [Rebalancing](/cesto/rebalancing) to learn how.
