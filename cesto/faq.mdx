**What is Cesto?**

Cesto is a platform for investing in narrative-driven baskets on Solana. Each basket is a curated portfolio of assets built around a specific theme or conviction — like defense spending, Congressional trading, or AI. You invest in one click, and the assets go directly to your wallet.

**How do I get started?**

Go to [app.cesto.co](https://app.cesto.co), connect your Solana wallet, browse the available baskets, and invest. That's it.

**What happens when I invest in a basket?**

You receive the underlying assets directly in your wallet. Not a token representing the basket — the actual assets, at the basket's target weights.

**Is my money safe?**

Cesto is fully self-custodial. Your assets stay in your wallet at all times. We never take custody of your funds, and you can exit any position whenever you want. [More on security](/cesto/security).

**What blockchain does Cesto run on?**

Solana.

**What are the returns shown on each basket?**

The annual return percentages are based on backtested historical performance over the past year. They show how the basket's strategy would have performed — not a guarantee of future results.

**What are RWAs?**

Real-world assets (RWAs) are tokenized versions of traditional assets like stocks and commodities, made accessible on-chain. Some baskets include RWAs alongside crypto-native tokens to give you exposure to both traditional and digital markets.

**Can I exit my position anytime?**

Yes. Your assets are in your wallet. You can sell any or all of them at any time — through Cesto or directly on any Solana DEX.

**What is rebalancing?**

When market movement causes your holdings to drift from the basket's target allocation, rebalancing adjusts your positions back to the intended weights. It's one click, and you approve every transaction. [Learn more](/cesto/rebalancing).

**Can I create my own basket?**

Not yet. Right now, Cesto offers curated baskets. We're working toward opening the platform to external curators and eventually to anyone who wants to build and share their own baskets.

**Do I need technical knowledge?**

No. If you know how to connect a wallet and approve a transaction, you can use Cesto.
