## Your positions, one view

After you invest in a basket, your [Portfolio](https://app.cesto.co/positions) shows everything in one place: which baskets you're in, what assets you hold, and how they're performing.

Because Cesto is non-custodial, the assets you see in your portfolio are the same assets sitting in your own wallet. There's no separate "Cesto balance": what you see is what you own.

## What you'll see

- **Active baskets**: Every basket you've invested in
- **Asset breakdown**: The individual assets in each basket and their current allocations
- **Performance**: How your positions are doing, including returns since you invested

To add funds or cash out, see [Funding](/cesto/funding).

## When allocations drift

As markets move, the assets in your basket will naturally shift away from their target weights. That's normal. When your positions drift far enough from the basket's intended strategy, you'll see rebalancing opportunities, a one-click way to realign.

More on that in [Rebalancing](/cesto/rebalancing).
