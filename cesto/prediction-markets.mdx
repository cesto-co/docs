## Beyond tokens

Most baskets hold assets: tokens and tokenized real-world assets. But a conviction isn't always "this asset goes up." Sometimes it's "this will happen." Will a rate cut land this quarter? Who wins the match? Does a bill pass?

Cesto baskets can include **prediction-market positions** alongside tokens, so a basket can express an outcome-based thesis, not just an asset allocation.

## How they work

Prediction-market positions are powered by **Polymarket**. Each one is a stake on a specific outcome: a market that resolves to a definite result on a set date.

When a basket includes a prediction-market leg:

- A slice of your investment buys into that outcome
- The position is held alongside the basket's token holdings
- When the market resolves, a correct outcome pays out; an incorrect one expires worthless

## In your portfolio

Prediction-market positions appear in your [Portfolio](https://app.cesto.co/positions) next to the basket's other holdings, with their current value. Because these markets resolve on the event's schedule, not continuously like a token, their value tracks the market's current odds until settlement.

When you close a position, or when it settles, the proceeds come back in USDC, just like the rest of the basket.

<Note>
Prediction markets resolve to a fixed outcome on a fixed date. Unlike a token you can hold indefinitely, a prediction position has an end: once the event resolves, the outcome is final.
</Note>

## Performance

Baskets that include prediction markets show performance just like any other basket, based on historical data, and never a guarantee of future results. Prediction legs are inherently event-driven: their result is decided when the market resolves.
