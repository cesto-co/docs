## Invest in what you believe

Cesto lets you invest in baskets of assets built around themes, narratives, and convictions. Defense spending is up? There's a basket for that. Congress is trading stocks? There's a basket tracking their moves. AI is overheated and smart money is rotating? Basket.

Each basket is a curated portfolio — a mix of crypto tokens, tokenized real-world assets (RWAs), and more — all accessible on Solana with a single click. No fund managers, no lock-ups, no custody risk. You invest, and the assets land directly in your wallet.

## Why baskets

Most people have a thesis. They read the news, follow markets, form opinions. But turning a thesis into a diversified portfolio across multiple asset classes? That's hard. It means researching individual assets, executing multiple trades, managing allocations, and staying on top of it all.

Cesto turns a conviction into a position. One click, and you own a diversified basket of assets that reflects exactly what you believe in.

## What's in a basket

Every basket is built around a specific narrative or strategy:

- **Political trading** — Track what Congress is buying, mirror Pelosi's disclosed positions, or bet against Jim Cramer
- **Geopolitical themes** — Defense contractors and military tech, or reshoring plays as manufacturing comes back to America
- **Market theses** — AI leaders, smart money rotation, high-growth momentum
- **Crypto-native** — Solana ecosystem tokens spanning DeFi, LSDs, and memecoins

Inside each basket, you'll find a transparent breakdown of every asset, its weight, and the thesis behind it. No black boxes.

## What makes Cesto different

- **Self-custodial.** Your assets sit in your wallet. Always. Cesto never takes custody of your funds.
- **One-click execution.** Complex, multi-asset portfolios executed in a single transaction on Solana.
- **Narrative-driven.** Baskets aren't random collections of tokens. Each one reflects a real thesis about where the world is heading.
- **Real-world assets on-chain.** Access tokenized stocks, commodities, and more — all from your Solana wallet.
- **Actively maintained.** Baskets aren't static. Positions are regularly updated, and you can rebalance to stay aligned with the strategy.

## What's ahead

Right now, Cesto offers curated baskets built and maintained by our team. Going forward, we'll onboard expert curators — analysts, traders, and strategists — who'll create and manage their own baskets on the platform. Eventually, anyone will be able to build and share baskets around their own convictions.

The goal is simple: if you have a thesis, you should be able to invest in it.
