## Build baskets on Cesto

Creators design and publish the investable baskets people put real money into. If you're an analyst, trader, or strategist with a thesis worth following, the creator program turns it into a product on Cesto.

This is a step beyond [Cesto Labs](/cesto/labs): Labs posts are community ideas, while creator baskets are fully-managed products, maintained over time.

## What you can do

- **Design a basket.** Choose tokens and set target weights for your strategy.
- **Tell the story.** Add the thesis, risk notes, and context so investors understand what they're backing.
- **Publish and maintain.** Publish your basket and keep it updated as your thesis evolves.

The creator program is currently access-gated. If you'd like to create baskets on Cesto, reach out via our [community](https://t.me/cesto_co) or email [jason@cesto.co](mailto:jason@cesto.co).
