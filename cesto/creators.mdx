## Build baskets on Cesto

Creators design and publish the investable baskets people put real money into. If you're an analyst, trader, or strategist with a thesis worth following, the creator program turns it into a product on Cesto.

This is a step beyond [Cesto Labs](/cesto/labs): Labs posts are community ideas, while creator baskets are fully-managed products: versioned, maintained over time, and eligible for fee sharing.

## What you can do

- **Design a basket.** Choose tokens (and prediction markets) and set target weights for your strategy.
- **Tell the story.** Add the thesis, risk notes, and context so investors understand what they're backing.
- **Publish and maintain.** Your basket goes live after review. As the thesis evolves, you ship new versions, and investors can rebalance into the latest allocation.
- **Earn.** Creators receive a share of the fees their baskets generate.

## How publishing works

1. **Draft.** Build your basket: allocation, metadata, and a cover image (you can even generate one with AI).
2. **Review.** New baskets and new versions start as drafts and are reviewed before going public.
3. **Live.** Once published, the basket appears in the catalog and anyone can invest.
4. **Rebalance.** Publish a new version when your strategy changes. Investors who've enabled auto-rebalance realign automatically; everyone else sees a [rebalance](/cesto/rebalancing) opportunity.
