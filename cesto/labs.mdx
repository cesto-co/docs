## What is Cesto Labs

[Cesto Labs](https://app.cesto.co/labs) is where the community builds. Anyone can publish a basket idea: a set of tokens with target weights and a short thesis. The community votes the best ones up.

It's the fastest way to go from "I have a thesis" to "here's my basket," without waiting to become a full creator.

## Browse the feed

The Labs feed lists community baskets you can sort by:

- **New**: the latest ideas
- **Trending**: what's getting attention right now
- **Top performers**: ranked by how the basket's allocation has performed

Open any post to see its full allocation, the author, its vote count, and an inline performance view.

## Vote

Back the baskets you believe in. Upvote the ideas you think are strong, and the most-backed baskets rise to the top. A creator leaderboard ranks authors by the votes they've earned.

## Publish your own

Creating a Labs post takes a minute:

1. Give it a title and a short description of the thesis
2. Add tokens with target weights
3. Make sure the allocation adds up to 100%
4. Publish, and it's live on the feed right away

You can edit or remove your own posts at any time.

<Note>
Labs posts are community basket *ideas*. To build a fully-managed, investable basket, see the [Creator program](/cesto/creators).
</Note>

## From your terminal

Prefer to work from your editor? The [Cesto Skill](/cesto/skill) can browse Labs, create posts, vote, and pull the leaderboard through natural conversation with your AI coding agent.
