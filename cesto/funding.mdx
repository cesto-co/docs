## Funding your account

Cesto investments are funded in **USDC** on Solana. Before you invest in a basket, you need USDC available to deploy across the basket's assets.

How you add funds depends on how you signed in:

- **Signed in with Google (embedded wallet).** Cesto created a wallet for you automatically. Send USDC to its address to top it up: copy the address from the deposit screen, or transfer in from an exchange or another wallet.
- **Connected an external wallet (Phantom, Solflare, …).** You invest from your own wallet's USDC balance. Just make sure it holds enough.

<Note>
USDC is the funding currency for every basket. If you hold other tokens, swap them to USDC first, on Cesto or any Solana DEX.
</Note>

## Depositing

Open the funding screen in the app, choose **Deposit**, and you'll see your Cesto wallet address (and a QR code). Send USDC to it from:

- A centralized exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.): withdraw USDC on the **Solana** network
- Another Solana wallet
- A bridge, if your USDC is on another chain

Your balance updates once the transfer confirms on Solana. From there, you're ready to invest.

## Withdrawing

You can take your money out at any time. Withdrawals are sent in **USDC** to any Solana address you choose.

1. Open the funding screen and choose **Withdraw**
2. Enter the amount and the destination Solana address
3. Authorize the withdrawal
4. The USDC lands in the destination wallet once it confirms on-chain

<Note>
Withdrawals are made in USDC. To withdraw the value of an open position, [close it](/cesto/my-portfolio) first. The basket's assets are swapped back to USDC, which you can then withdraw.
</Note>

## Good to know

- **USDC in, USDC out.** Funding and withdrawals both use USDC on Solana.
- **No lock-ups.** Your funds are never locked. Close positions and withdraw whenever you want.
- **Check the network.** When depositing from an exchange, always select **Solana** and send **USDC**. The wrong asset or network can mean lost funds.
